About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Artificial Intelligence Thrust at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), supervised by Dr. Jintai Chen. My current research explores trustworthy and clinically grounded AI, including medical image analysis, treatment subgroup discovery, drug discovery, and rule mining for tabular data.

I earned my Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Xiamen University, where I was advised by Prof. Min Jiang. During my master's studies, I worked on trustworthy AI algorithms, with a focus on security, robustness, interpretability, and fairness for multimodal and graph learning systems.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Guangzhou University of Chinese Medicine, where I was advised by Prof. Wu Zhou. My academic work is driven by a long-term interest in bridging machine learning methodology with high-stakes applications, especially clinical medicine and biomedical discovery.

Research Experience

AI for Healthcare and Scientific Discovery

Supervisor: Dr. Jintai Chen | Nov. 2025 - present

Developing clinically grounded AI methods for 3D bone collapse prediction, interpretable treatment subgroup discovery, tabular rule mining, and molecular recovery from single-target diffusion models.

Security and Robustness of AI Algorithms

Supervisor: Professor Min Jiang | Feb. 2023 - Feb. 2025

This research reveals how attackers can subtly and efficiently alter AI outputs, including image captioning and 3DGS watermarking, without internal model access.

Interpretability and Fairness of AI Algorithms

Supervisor: Professor Min Jiang | Sep. 2022 - Feb. 2024

This research examines how subgraph patterns cause bias in graph neural networks and develops diagnostic, actionable explanations for fair graph learning.

AI Algorithms for Clinical Tumor Characterization

Supervisor: Professor Wu Zhou | Sep. 2020 - Jun. 2022

This research exposes the shortcomings of conventional mathematical models in DWI and DCE-MRI for tumor analysis, proposing an AI model with spatiotemporal attention for direct prediction.
